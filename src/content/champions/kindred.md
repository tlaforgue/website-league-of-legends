## Kindred clear

### Red (2:47)

1. Lvl W first and use it right before red spawns. Kite arround
2. Save W and once red dies, AA > Q > AA > W big krug. Kill small krugs with Q and AA
3. W on raptors and kite to patience
4. Save A and lvl E when raptors dies
5. Q > W over the wolf wall
6. E when big wolf is arround 600 HP
7. Leave patience when E empowered AA is through. AA each little wolves
8. Q on Gromp and kite to patience
9. E when gromp is arround 600 HP.
10. Kite blue to patience. E whan arround 600 HP

### Blue (2:48)

1. Lvl W first and use it right before blue spawns. Kite to patience
2. Once W comes up, 2 AA and 2 strikes from W will kill blue with Smite.
3. W then Q ASAP
4. Kite to patience
5. Kite gromp when arround 350 HP
6. Kite wolfs to patience
7. Kite away when Wolf below 300 HP
8. Lvl E and Q over raptors wall. Use W ASAP. E when raptors is half HP
9. Kite away whan Raptor is below 100 HP
10. Kite red above krugs wall
11. Q over wall. W and Q back and target red. E red when 600 HP
