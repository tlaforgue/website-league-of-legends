## Kayn clear

### Red (2:48)

1. Open AA > Q > Smite. Kite towards krugs
2. W to hit both krugs
3. AA > Q into wall. Kite towards raptors
4. AA mini krug then finish with W
5. AA the nearest raptor then Q through camp. Kite towards bush. Finish with W and AA
6. E through wolf wall > AA > Q. Kite to blue
7. W > AA twice > Q
8. Wolf under 200 HP > Blue > Q through both
9. AA > Q > AA against wall at the edge of patience
10. W gromp when blue under 200 HP. AA blue to finish
11. Finish gromp with AA > Q > AA

### Blue (2:43)

1. Open AA > Q > Smite. Kite to patience. AA > Q > AA against wall
2. Kite to gromp when blue under 150 HP
3. W gromp > AA > Q > AA
4. Kite gromp to patience
5. Kito to wolves when gromp under 400 HP. Finish with AA > Q  AA
6. AA > Q  AA wolves. Kite towards raptors and W
7. Wolf under 400 HP > kite to raptors > AA > Q
8. E raptor wall > AA > W > Q. Kite to red
9. Raptor under 400 HP > kite to red > AA > W
10. Open AA > AA > W > Q. Kite towards bush
11. AA red below 100 HP > E over wall > AA krug
