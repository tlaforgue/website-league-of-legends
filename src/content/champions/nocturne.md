## Nocturne clear

### Red (2:42)

1. Q from behind raptors
2. 2nd Q cast downwards. Kite towards red
3. Lvl W > Q to krugs
4. One medium krug dies ang big is under 100 HP > walk to red to Q it. Finish big with Passive. Finish mini krugs with another passive
5. Lvl Q > Q towards wolves
6. Q wolves (2nd Q towards Gromp)
7. Q gromp > kite towards blue
8. (hardest part) When Q up > Q blue. Make sure to not lose patience > Land Passive on both > Walk to blue patience
9. (hardest part) AOE both using Passive and Q > AA gromp more if necessary

### Blue (2:33)

1. Q from behind blue towards gromp > repeat
2. Q through blue to hit gromp > walk to gromp when blue under 100 > finish with passive
3. Final Q towards wolves
4. Q on wolves > 2nd Q towards raptors
5. Lvl Q and use it ASAP
6. Q on red > finish raptors with Passive
7. Q to drag krugs > walk to krugs when red arround 300
8. Finish red with Passive and Q
