## Rek'Sai clear

### Red (2:52)

1. Q > AA on raptors
2. Lvl W > Smite red > W > Q
3. When red under 500 > WQ to leash krugs
4. Finish krugs then Recall & buy Long Sword
5. WQ wolves and tunnel tthrough wall. Kite to patience
6. Leash gromp with WQ. Kite to patience
7. WQ to leash blue

### Blue (2:44)

1. AA > Q > Smite blue. Kite to patience
2. Kite out when blue under 200
3. AA gromp > Q > AA blue to hit both
4. Lvl E and use it ASAP. Kite to patience
5. Kite out when gromp under 300
6. E wolf then AA the closest. A spare AA on each small wolves. Kite to patience
7. Kite out when wolf under 250
8. Lvl W and WQ raptors ASAP > Tunnel
9. When Q comes up > WQ red to leash. Kite raptors when under 100
10. WQ to leash krugs
11. WQ + pet to finish small krugs
